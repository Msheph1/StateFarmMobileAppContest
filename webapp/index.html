How it works?

Looking for the perfect place to eat but having no luck, we have just the thing for you! First you will select whether you are looking for a group or yourself. Then you will pick which filters apply best to you. Next, we will give you several suggestions that you will say whether or not you are intersted in. Lastly, we have found the perfect place for your next meal!

About us

img

This applicaiton was created with one goal in mind to help people decide what to eat and where. Truly is always a struggle when you have so many great options, espically with a group of people. This applicaiton aims to provide a fun game like way of finding which resturant to eat at. This application is also being created in a mobile environment, where we believe it really shines! Currently only available for Android devices, other devices will be coming soon
